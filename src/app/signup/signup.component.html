About Kysms

Kysms is a service where you can text any question to a specific phone number and receive a prompt and accurate answer from AI. It's quick, easy, and always available when you need it!

The Name "Kysms"

The name "Kysms" is inspired by the Finnish word for question, "kysymys", and SMS, the traditional text messaging service. It’s a blend that represents the essence of what we offer—a quick, reliable way to get answers to your questions through messaging.
